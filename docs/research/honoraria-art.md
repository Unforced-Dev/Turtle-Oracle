# Research: BRC 2026 Honoraria Art (theme "Axis Mundi")

75 funded projects, ~$1.3M, 15 countries (official Journal announcement, ~March 2026). Jury got **41 tree-themed proposals, selected six**, plus mountain- and body-themed work. All pieces below are HIGH confidence for being funded; **on-playa placement (deep playa vs. near the Man) is NOT yet published** — treat "at night / climb it" dare specifics as plausible intent, not confirmed logistics.

## Named 2026 pieces (most deck-relevant)
- **Axis Mundi: Resonant Spire** — Sergei Konchekov — 33-ft wooden tower that captures voices and turns them into light and sound. *(The literal theme piece — and a physical mirror of our voice-oracle.)*
- **Yggdrasil** — Morten Ørtenblad — Norse World Tree from community-grown bamboo.
- **Above and Below** — Sean Orlando — 40-ft immersive treehouse joined by a beanstalk connector.
- **Elemental Dragon** — Arterra Techtonics — 9×40-ft dragon built from branches and roots.
- **Headwaters** — MSALIGNED Creative — bamboo meditation labyrinth inspired by Mount Kailash.
- **Freedom Dancers** — Ashley Stoddard — seven female figures with branch-like arms (community-built).
- **Mebuyan Pulse** — Leeroy New et al. — climbable suspended spheres; Mebuyan, Bagobo underworld/nurturer goddess.
- **Heart Remains** — Giselle Çisel Çakıroğlu — glowing ribcage with a suspended heart.
- **PULSE** — Manuela Magni — cracked heart pulsing light, "Earth's heartbeat."
- **Behind Closed Eyes** — Spencer Hansen — reclining topographic figure, Balinese/Javanese hand-tool texture.
- **QUEEN: Quantum Universal Epic Era Now!** — Sophi Kravitz, Ollie Tanner — reclining midlife woman; an "invisibilized" life stage.
- **TITANIC** — Titanic's End — 200-ft sinking ship broken in half.
- **For They Know Not What They Do** — Jai Hackl — 21-ft steel caduceus (world-axis symbol).
- **Seven Sisters** — Caleb Nederhood — illuminated Pleiades star cluster.
- **The Sanctuary of the Silent Star** — Michael McIntosh — lotus cocoon activated by *quiet*, not sound.
- **Open Arms** — Jorge E. Martinez — large soft sculpture, outstretched welcoming arms.
- **The Circle of Ancestors** — Maurice Cavness — (desc. not yet released; on-theme for Roots).
- **Kaleidoscope** — Studio Woo Woo — 500 fused-glass monarch butterflies (migration/impermanence).
- **Tree of Photon** — Artur Grycuk — tree light piece (desc. pending).
- **The Wishing Tree** — Rockford Revelry — (desc. pending).
- Others in the 75: CORREFOC, Fire Gnome, Octopolis, LOOKOUT!, Carnac 2026, The Royal Trumpets, The Council of Animals.

## Card seeds (12 strongest)
```
1. Axis Mundi: Resonant Spire — being heard; what we speak echoes back changed. Shell. Dare: speak a wish/a name into the spire at night, watch your voice become light. [high]
2. Yggdrasil — lineage; you're held by roots you didn't plant. Shell. Dare: touch the trunk, name your lineage aloud, leave an offering for who comes after. [high]
3. Above and Below — reaching up requires standing somewhere. Branches. Dare: climb at sunrise; at top name what you reach for, at base name what roots you. [high]
4. Mebuyan Pulse — descent as sacred; the underworld tends us. Roots. Dare: climb after dark; honor one hard descent you've survived as holy ground. [high]
5. Heart Remains — grief; the body remembers. Roots. Dare: sit inside the ribcage, say aloud the name of someone lost. [high]
6. PULSE — endurance through brokenness; a broken thing still beats. Trunk. Dare: rest against it, slow your breath to match the pulse. [high]
7. Headwaters — pilgrimage inward; the way out is through the center. Branches. Dare: walk the labyrinth silent; enter with a question, leave the answer at center. [high]
8. Behind Closed Eyes — the inner landscape. Roots. Dare: close your eyes, trace the figure by touch, notice what appears in the dark. [high]
9. TITANIC — impermanence, hubris, the dignity of letting go. Roots. Dare: at the wreck at night, release one thing you grip — write it, leave it, let it sink. [high]
10. QUEEN — being seen; worth of the un-spectacular middle of a life. Trunk. Dare: tell someone in an "invisible" season you see them; then recline, be gloriously un-busy. [high]
11. Freedom Dancers — collective liberation; we branch toward each other. Branches. Dare: dance with the figures at golden hour, pull a stranger in as one more branch. [high]
12. Seven Sisters — kinship, chosen family. Branches. Dare: lie beneath at dawn, find the real Pleiades, silently name your "sisters." [high]
```
Strong alternates: For They Know Not What They Do (caduceus — Shell/Trunk), Elemental Dragon (roots↔branches), The Sanctuary of the Silent Star (Trunk), The Circle of Ancestors (Roots).
