# Research: Terrible Turtle Camp

## What they are
A young Burning Man theme camp at the intersection of **AI, art, and community**. 2026 is their **"round two"** (their proto-year was **"Terrible Turtle Camp #0"**). Founder/lead: **Jade** (Longmont, CO); community members incl. **Ash Howe**, **Jeremy Wood**. Site is aspirational/recruiting-stage (homepage + application form + one linked event); not yet in directory.burningman.org.

## ⭐ The key finding
At their **June 2026 gathering they "discussed an AI tarot initiative for the festival."** → This oracle deck is the **realization of a thing the camp already wanted to build.** Position it as fulfilling their own idea. Their framing ("edge of emerging technology and art," "AI and spirituality," "code becomes canvas") gives us permission to be both mystical and machine-native.

## Verbatim voice (use these in the deck)
- Motto: **"Move Slow & Bite Things"** (also footer: "© 2026 Terrible Turtle Camp. Move Slow & Bite Things.")
- Belonging: **"Whether you weld, code, cook, or just want to make something weird in the desert — there's a spot in the shell for you."**
- Identity: **"code becomes canvas and the desert becomes our studio."**
- Section voice: **"Ready to Bite?"**, **"Stay in the Shell"** (newsletter), "Most Recent Events."
- App copy: "early pricing disappears faster than your campmates' sunscreen."

## Phoenix Gulch (their land project) — the Roots realm made literal
June 20, 2026 gathering near Longmont, CO. Several hundred acres. Plans: **"carve water management terraces to pull moisture back into the dry ridges, plant drought-tolerant trees and shrubs,"** and **"bring native grasses back to life."** Regeneration, stewardship, patient repair. (In a World-Tree year, this camp both *carries* the tree and *plants* trees.)

## Aesthetic (creative latitude — nothing locked)
Turtle mascot/logo; desert/studio setting; conservation/nature undertone (green/earth/desert palette); playful-menacing snapping-turtle attitude. No published colors or defined turtle art style.

## Card seeds — Terrible-Turtle honor cards
```
1. Move Slow & Bite Things — discernment + decisive action; the slow yes and the sharp no. Realm: Trunk. Dare: the thing you've circled for weeks — bite it today, fully. [high]
2. There's a Spot in the Shell — belonging; you don't have to be loudest or credentialed. Realm: Shell. Dare: invite someone who thinks they don't belong; make room before you feel ready. [high]
3. Code Becomes Canvas — tech as artistic medium; the algorithm is a color you haven't mixed. Realm: Branches. Dare: take a work tool and make something useless and beautiful. [high]
4. The Desert Becomes Our Studio — transform your conditions; make the harsh place fertile. Realm: Roots. Dare: name the "desert" in your life; bring one creative act into it. [med-high]
5. Bring Native Grasses Back to Life (Phoenix Gulch) — restoration, stewardship, patient repair. Realm: Roots. Dare: tend something you've let go dry, without expecting fast results. [med-high]
6. Make Something Weird — permission to be strange; create without justification. Realm: Branches. Dare: build the weird thing you're embarrassed to want; show exactly one person. [high]
7. The Oracle at the Edge (meta) — nod to their own AI-tarot idea; the machine dreams alongside you, doesn't answer for you. Realm: Shell. Dare: ask the oracle one question you're afraid to answer — then answer it yourself first. [med]
```
