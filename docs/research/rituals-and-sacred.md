# Research: BRC 2026 Rituals, Temple, Man & Sacred Elements

## Confirmed 2026 sacred structures (HIGH confidence)

### The Temple — "Temple of the Moon"
- **Artist:** James Gwertzman + Moonlight Collective / Temple Build Crew.
- **Concept:** the **Queen of the Night** — a desert cactus flower that blooms for a single night, releases fragrance under the moon, fades by dawn. Metaphor for impermanence, grief, release.
- **Form:** ~170 ft diameter; organic curves from straight timber (parametric design); slatted petals radiating from center (flower-shaped from above); **eight moon-phase gateways**; five archways into a cathedral chamber; a **52-ft central vertical "stamen"** — a literal vertical axis-mundi. Crown petals hinge open at sunset, close at sunrise. Build timed to the lunar cycle; burns as the moon returns to new (Sunday night, silent).
- Quote: honors "the many ways grief and gratitude are experienced"; celebrates "impermanence and immediacy."

### The Man — "Cryptomeria"
- **Designer:** Alexander "Zander" Rose.
- **Concept:** *Cryptomeria japonica* (Japanese cedar / Sugi), specifically the ancient **Jōmon Sugi** (~2,170–7,200 yrs old) — an explicit World-Tree / axis-mundi choice.
- **Feature:** an **outer spiral staircase** rises ~60 ft; an **inner spiral staircase** descends — together a **double helix (DNA)** = past, present, future. Burns Saturday night (celebration, renewal).

### Dates
Black Rock City, NV. Aug 30 – Sep 6/7, 2026 (sources vary; Labor Day = Mon Sep 7). Man burns Saturday; Temple burns Sunday.

> **Both 2026 sacred structures are literal World Trees** — the Temple's 52-ft central stamen and the Man's ancient-cedar/double-helix trunk are both vertical axis-mundi forms. Perfect Shell-card anchors.
