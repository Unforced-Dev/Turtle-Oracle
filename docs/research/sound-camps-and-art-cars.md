# Research: BRC 2026 Sound Camps & Art Cars

Burning Man publishes no central music lineup (music is discovered, not scheduled); camps announce their own lineups mid-to-late summer. The **official 2026 Mutant Vehicle registry is live**, so several art cars are already confirmed. Recurring institutions rate HIGH even without a 2026 lineup because they return every year.

| Name | Type | Vibe | 2026 confidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Robot Heart** | art car (double-decker bus, glowing heart) | the definitive deep-playa **sunrise set** | HIGH (registry cites it) |
| **Mayan Warrior / "Galaxyer"** | art car (Mexico) | Mayan spaceship-altar, lasers + world-class underground; reborn after 2023 fire | HIGH (touring into 2026) |
| **Distrikt** | camp (SF) | largest **daytime** sound camp, up to 3,000/day, inclusive house/techno | HIGH |
| **Opulent Temple** | camp (since 2003) | ~50m sphere stage, DJ-controlled **flame columns**; house/techno cathedral | HIGH |
| **El Pulpo Mecánico** (& El Pulpo Magnífico) | art car | 26-ft scrap-metal **fire-breathing octopus** | HIGH (Magnífico in 2026 registry) |
| **Kalliope** | art car (AZ) | neon steam-circus calliope; wall of subs, fire | MED-HIGH |
| **Dusty Rhino** | art car (Oakland) | glowing rhino, fire + bass; **funds real rhino conservation** | MED-HIGH |
| **Pink Mammoth** | camp (SF) | riotously pink **daytime** oasis; shade, drinks, funky/disco house | MED-HIGH |
| **White Ocean** | camp | ethereal all-white dreamworld stage | MED |
| **Space Cowboys — UNIMOG** | art car (since 2001) | the **original** big sound truck; ~16,000W; the elder statesman | MED |
| **Root Society** | camp | massive dome + tipi sound village | MED |
| **Deco Fish Twins** | art car(s) | twin Art-Deco deep-sea fish, LED + flame + 20kW | HIGH (in 2026 registry) |

Also confirmed in 2026 registry: BAAAHS (disco sheep), Gon KiRin (fire dragon), Abraxas, Airpusher, Fjörgyn (Viking longboat), Don't Panic, ¡AXOLOTL!.
