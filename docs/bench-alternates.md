# Bench / Alternates — strong cards not in the 48

All researched, real, and deck-worthy — kept here for easy swaps if we want to trade any card out. Grouped by likely realm.

## Shell (archetype)
- **Black Rock Bakery** — the best gifts have a price that isn't money; nourishment traded for honesty. *Dare: trade a true, hard-to-say story for a warm pastry.* [high]
- **White Ocean** — begin again in white; reinvention is a clean canvas. *Dare: wear all white, disappear into the set as the sky changes.* [med]

## Roots (face / release)
- **The Circle of Ancestors** (art, Maurice Cavness) — who and what you descend from. [high; full desc. pending]
- **Elemental Dragon** (art) — built literally from roots and branches; the beast made of your whole self. [high]
- **Space Cowboys / UNIMOG** (art car) — honor the elders; roots run under the newest bloom. [med]
- **Dusty Rhino** (art car) — play with purpose; revelry can carry a cause. [med-high]

## Trunk (stand / present)
- **The Sanctuary of the Silent Star** (art, Michael McIntosh) — a lotus cocoon activated by *quiet*, not sound; the power of stillness. [high]
- **Consent Culture / B.E.D.** — yes means yes; consent is rehearsed, not assumed. *(Currently folded into "The Down Low Club.")* [high archetype]

## Branches (reach / connect)
- **Freedom Dancers** (art, Ashley Stoddard) — seven figures with branch-like arms; collective liberation. [high]
- **Kaleidoscope** (art, Studio Woo Woo) — 500 fused-glass monarch butterflies; migration & impermanence. [high]
- **The Bell at the Gate** (ritual) — mark your thresholds; every real beginning deserves a sound. [high]
- **Ecstatic Dance at Dawn** (ritual) — dance your prayer at the edge of night. [high] *(overlaps Robot Heart / Opulent Temple)*
- **Kalliope** (art car) — announce yourself; some are heard before they're seen. [med-high]
- **Pink Mammoth** (camp) — tend the daytime; rest and unhurried joy are their own gift. [med-high]
